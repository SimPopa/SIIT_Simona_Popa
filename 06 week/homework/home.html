Welcome to the robbery game! The goal is to make your way to the money, and not get caught by police You can move in all four directions, using the arrow keys on your keyboard. There are 5 policemen, hidden randomly across the board. If you encounter one of them, you lose one life. After 3 police encounters, you lose and the game will restart Good Luck!
